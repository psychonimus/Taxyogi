Who we are

Get to know about Dashpute and Associates

Dashpute and Associates is an Accounting, Auditing and Consulting firm having a rich blend of heritage of 36+ years and aspiration of a growing firm, which provides tailor made solutions to satisfy the clients' needs through flawless and dedicated process, technology, and innovation. Headquartered in Pune, registered with Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).

We provide a complete suite of advisory, assurance and tax services, encompassing planning, compliance, certification and representation, from a domestic as well as international perspective. We provide integrated solutions to multinationals and privately held businesses.
