MSME Success Summit 2024

The MSME Success Summit 24, organized by Dashpute and Associates in partnership with TAXYOGI Fin Advisory P. Ltd., achieved significant success with over 100 attendees. The event featured presentations on Tax Updates, Funding Pathways, and Growth Strategies by 8 distinguished guest speakers. The speakers, including CA Abhishek Dashpute and CA Kamlesh Purohit, Tuljapurkar Sohal Thakur Ravi Ghate Rohit & Ram Nayar Samruddhi Puranik shared valuable insights to help MSMEs navigate the economic landscape and enhance business growth. It wouldnt have had happend without all the attendees so Thank you everyone for your enthusiastic participation. Keep an eye out for upcoming events and initiatives customised to empower MSMEs!
