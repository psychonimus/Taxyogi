Meet the Founder

CA Abhishek Dashpute

Inspired by the thought he firmly believes "If you change your mindset, you can change this world the way you want", He heads the Firm "Dashpute and Associates" which is inherited by the legacy of his Father "D R Dashpute" who started this business nearly four decades now. Abhishek joined the Business of Consultancy even before qualifying as a Chartered Accountant. He has also been in this profession for almost a decade now. Having hands-on experience in the Consultancy Business since the very start of his career, he understood the need to be niche and versatile due to which he is making changes in the way of Serving the esteemed Clientele. He spearheads the division of Audit and Assurance. Now, considering the growing needs of the Industry, he is aspiring to make this firm a "Multi-Dimensional Firm" having diversified Partners for different departments altogether.

Our Vision

To offer our clients a comprehensive range of financial consulting and compliance services, directing them towards a financially secure future.

Our Mission

Our goal at Dashpute & Associates is to offer a comprehensive range of financial services and make the process of filing Income Tax returns simplified and hassle-free. We are dedicated to enhancing our clients' financial literacy and building trust with them. Dashpute and Associates' mission is to deliver all finance-related services in one convenient location.
